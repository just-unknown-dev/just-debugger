# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

The **just_debugger** project is committed to providing a respectful, welcoming, and harassment-free environment for contributors and maintainers.

We expect everyone participating in the project to act professionally, constructively, and with empathy.

---

## Expected Behavior

Examples of positive behavior include:

- being respectful and inclusive
- accepting feedback professionally
- focusing on technical collaboration
- helping maintain a healthy project environment

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- harassment or intimidation
- insulting, abusive, or discriminatory language
- personal attacks
- sharing private information without permission
- repeated disruptive behavior

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to project discussions, issues, pull requests, reviews, and other community interactions related to **just_debugger**.

---

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions and interactions that violate this Code of Conduct.

Serious or repeated violations may result in temporary or permanent exclusion from project participation.

---

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, report it to the project maintainers through the repository communication channels.

All reports should be handled respectfully and with appropriate privacy.
